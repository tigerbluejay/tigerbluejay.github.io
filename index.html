Welcome to my website!

I am a software developer, and a lifelong learner, with a mixed background in political science, economics, foreign languages, mathematics and computer science.

Currently (mid-2025), I'm developing Angular client/.NET API applications as well as .NET microservice infrastructure projects. This is part of my ongoing effort to stay current with my professional stack after spending several years away from formal employment as a developer. I plan to resume full-time work as a developer toward the end of the year.

I'm also handling some home renovations and fixtures, and I plan to return to the gym to jog, stretch, and lift once I recover from upcoming dental surgery. To improve my voice and presence, I'm taking weekend courses in voice projection and body language. 2025 has already been a productive year—a step forward for me in many respects—though there’s still plenty to do before I take on greater challenges in 2026.

On a more personal note, I enjoy spending time with my family—especially my niece and nephews, parents, and siblings. I also like jogging and stretching in my free time, managing my finances, and casually staying up to date with social media. As an avid learner, I enjoy reading about psychology and personal growth, particularly in the areas of negotiation, personality, nutrition, exercise, and both personal and professional strategy.
